Restaurants – AI Playbook

Fill peak times, lift spend per guest, and keep diners coming back

What this page gives you

This playbook shows where AI helps a restaurant today. It focuses on booking fill, demand planning, and repeat diners.

Use the 30 Day AI Flywheel: Identify Cycle Measure Scale.

Typical Growth Drivers

  • Keep tables full at peak times.
  • Increase average spend per guest.
  • Improve delivery and takeaway margins.
  • Retain loyal diners and re activate lapsed ones.

AI Use Cases Already Working

  • AI booking and waitlist assistant by web and WhatsApp.
  • Menu optimisation and simple dynamic pricing for specials.
  • Predictive demand for staffing and stock to cut waste.
  • Automated loyalty offers by SMS or email.
  • Post visit review prompts to grow local visibility.

Data Readiness (What you need)

  • Reservation and POS history with time stamps.
  • Delivery and takeaway order data.
  • Staff rota and shift logs.
  • Consent and opt in data for messaging.

CSV export is enough to start. If your system has an API, use it later to automate.

Metrics to Track ROI

  • Table occupancy rate during peak and off peak.
  • Average revenue per cover.
  • Food waste percentage.
  • Repeat diner rate.
  • Delivery time average and order accuracy.

Example 30 Day Focus

  • Week 1: add an AI booking and waitlist assistant. Enable SMS and email confirmations and reminders.
  • Week 2: set up loyalty reminders for recent diners with a simple return offer valid mid week.
  • Week 3: pilot AI demand forecasting to plan staff and key ingredients for the next two weekends.
  • Week 4: review occupancy and average spend per cover vs last month. Keep the two best changes and drop the rest.

What “good” looks like

  • Peak table occupancy up 5 to 10 points.
  • Average spend per cover up by £2 to £6.
  • Food waste down by 10 to 20 percent.
  • More mid week bookings and repeat diners.

Keep it simple. One change per week. Measure, then scale what works.

Set up in under an hour

  • Export the last 6 to 12 months of reservations and POS.
  • Switch on automated confirmations and reminders.
  • Add booking and waitlist assistant to your site and WhatsApp.

If you use SevenRooms, OpenTable, ResDiary or similar, use built in features first.

Fast wins to test

  • Return diner offer valid Monday to Thursday only.
  • Dynamic specials based on stock and predicted demand.
  • Auto send review links two hours after the bill is closed.

Guardrails

  • Get consent for marketing messages and make opt out easy.
  • Keep data inside your booking and POS systems.
  • Do not over discount. Use small nudges and time windows.
