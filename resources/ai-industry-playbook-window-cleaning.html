Window Cleaning AI Playbook

Fill the route raise average order value and reduce no shows

What this page gives you

This playbook shows where AI helps a window cleaning business today. It focuses on steady recurring routes smart upsells faster quotes and clear messages that cut phone time and boost on time payment.

Use the 30 Day AI Flywheel: Identify Cycle Measure Scale.

Typical Growth Drivers

  • Increase new sign ups for monthly bi monthly and quarterly cleans.
  • Lift add ons frames and sills inside glass conservatories skylights fascia and gutter and solar panels.
  • Reduce no shows and late cancels with friendly reminders deposits and easy reschedule.
  • Cut quote time with photo based pricing and simple property questions.
  • Grow route density so crews spend more time cleaning and less time driving.
  • Raise on time payment with links and automatic nudges.

AI Use Cases Already Working

  • Booking and quote assistant on web and WhatsApp that collects address photos property size access notes pets and preferred frequency.
  • Instant quote helper that maps photos and answers to your price table and suggests add ons like inside glass and frames.
  • Smart reminders with prep move ornaments close windows unlock side gate and confirm parking.
  • Weather aware reschedule that offers next best slots when heavy rain or high winds hit.
  • Route optimiser that clusters nearby stops balances ladders and water fed pole work and hits service windows.
  • Payment chaser with gentle tone and one tap links that sync to your ledger.
  • Review and referral prompts with before and after photos and shareable links.
  • Answer bank for safety stains water marks and what the service includes and excludes.

Data Readiness What you need

  • Job history by address date frequency status crew time taken and materials like pure water and scrapers.
  • Quote logs with photos price and win or loss reason.
  • Customer list with postcode consent channel and payment method.
  • Access notes gate codes dogs ladder limits and parking.
  • Payment ledger invoices paid overdue and credits.
  • Route maps and travel time plus weather notes for missed days.
  • Price table by house type window count height and add ons.

Start with CSV exports. Keep customer data private and only use what you need for each step.

Metrics to Track ROI

  • Lead to first clean rate and time to first reply.
  • Average order value and add on attach rate.
  • Route density stops per hour and miles per stop.
  • No show and cancel rate with deposit coverage.
  • On time payment rate and days sales outstanding.
  • Churn and repeat interval adherence.
  • Review score and referral rate.
  • Weather recovery rate percent of missed work rebooked within seven days.

Example 30 Day Focus

  • Week 1: add a booking and quote assistant on site and WhatsApp. Turn on confirmations deposits and reminders with easy reschedule.
  • Week 2: load last 12 months of customers jobs and payments. Enable photo quotes with a checklist by house type and window count.
  • Week 3: publish simple bundles outside only outside plus frames outside and inside plus conservatory roof clean with clear inclusions.
  • Week 4: review lead to first clean average order value route density no shows on time payment and reviews versus last month. Keep the best two wins.

What good looks like

  • Lead to first clean rate up by 15 to 25 percent.
  • Average order value up by 10 to 20 percent from add ons and bundles.
  • No shows down by 25 to 40 percent with deposits and reminders.
  • Stops per hour up through better clustering and slot planning.
  • On time payment above 95 percent with automatic nudges.

Keep it simple. One change per week. Measure then scale what works.

Set up in under an hour

  • Export customers jobs payments and quotes to CSV with address frequency and access notes.
  • Switch on confirmations deposits reminders and payment links.
  • Add the assistant to your site and WhatsApp with a clear call to action and a QR on vans door hangers and cards.

If you use Google Calendar a payments tool and a simple CRM you can start with built in reports and message tools.

Fast wins to test

  • Neighbor deal two or more homes on the same street discount on the extra stops.
  • Inside glass add on every third visit prompt at booking.
  • Rain day gap fill texts to nearby postcodes with small perk.
  • End of build sparkle clean with a premium price list and safety checklist.

Guardrails

  • Do not work at unsafe heights or in high winds. If risk is high reschedule.
  • Follow ladder rules use cones and spotters where needed.
  • Protect privacy. Keep addresses photos and gate codes restricted.
  • Get consent for messages and make opt out easy.
  • Set honest limits. Stain removal scratches or hard water marks might need specialist work.

Integration Pathways

  • Scheduling: Google Calendar or Outlook with shared crew calendars.
  • Routing: map tools for clustering and travel time.
  • Messaging: WhatsApp Business SMS and email for reminders and updates.
  • Payments: links for deposits and pay on completion with receipt flow.
  • Reviews: automated asks with photo proof and links.

Adopt step by step. You do not need to replace current tools to start.

AI Build Options

  • Low code: n8n or Zapier for lead capture reminders payment nudges and review asks.
  • Chat layer: a guarded assistant that quotes from your price table bundles and rules and triggers deposits.
  • Vision: photo intake that estimates window count height and risk and flags ladder limits.
  • Forecasting: weather aware slot planning and seasonality dashboards.
  • Dashboards: Google Sheets Looker Studio or Power BI for weekly review.

Keep messages short clear and tied to one action.

Staff Workflow Impact

  • Fewer back and forth quotes as photos and rules set scope upfront.
  • Better day plans from clustered routes and realistic time bands.
  • Less phone time and more paid work on site.
  • Cleaner handoffs with access notes and payment status in one place.

Cost vs Return

  • Lead to first clean up 20 percent grows revenue without more ads.
  • No shows down 30 percent protects the day value.
  • Route density up means more stops per hour and lower fuel per stop.
  • On time payment up improves cash flow and cuts admin.

Most tools cost far less than the gains above.

Customer Experience Gains

  • Fast quotes with clear options and honest limits.
  • Simple reschedule and deposit links that do not need a call.
  • On time arrivals thanks to smart routing and access checks.
  • Photo proof of work with care tips for frames and sills.

Business Readiness Checklist

  • 12 months of jobs quotes and payments exportable to CSV.
  • Price table by house type height and add ons.
  • Template messages for reminders reschedule payment and reviews.
  • WhatsApp Business or SMS sender set up.
  • Named owner for weekly review of results.

If you tick most of these you can start this week.

Future Plays 12 to 24 months

  • Voice notes to job record and automatic photo tagging by window type.
  • Seasonal demand forecasting to plan routes offers and staffing.
  • Vision checks that score finish quality from photos for quality control.
  • Dynamic pricing that balances travel time weather risk and slot fill rate.
  • Light subscription with preferred time windows and loyalty perks.

Case Example

  • Two van team serving a town and nearby villages.
  • Turned on photo quotes deposits weather aware reschedule and payment nudges.
  • Lead to first clean up 21 percent in six weeks.
  • No shows down 32 percent and stops per hour up by 12 percent.
  • On time payment up to 96 percent with fewer chase calls.

Results will vary. The weekly review locks in wins and drops weak ideas.
