Off Licences AI Playbook

Grow basket size protect margin reduce stockouts and cut waste

What this page gives you

This playbook shows where AI helps an off licence today. It focuses on bigger baskets, better chilled availability, fewer stockouts on hero lines, and quicker answers that keep queues moving.

Use the 30 Day AI Flywheel: Identify Cycle Measure Scale.

Typical Growth Drivers

  • Increase average basket with simple add ons snacks mixers ice and cups.
  • Lift weekend and event sales with weather and calendar aware ordering.
  • Reduce stockouts on beer wine spirits and soft drinks.
  • Cut write offs on short date chilled and seasonal lines.
  • Use staff time better with cleaner rotas and fewer routine queries.

AI Use Cases Already Working

  • Demand forecasts by SKU day and weather to guide orders and space.
  • Replen alerts that factor lead times promos and sports events.
  • Till prompts for add ons tonic cola crisps nuts ice and bags.
  • Quiet time offers for mixers and snacks sent by WhatsApp or SMS to regulars.
  • FAQ assistant for opening hours delivery age check rules and local licensing notices.
  • Loss alerts from exception reports for voids refunds and no scans.

Data Readiness What you need

  • POS sales by date time SKU price quantity discount and channel.
  • On hand stock by SKU last delivery cost and supplier lead time.
  • Promo calendar sports events bank holidays local festivals.
  • Customer list with consent flags for email SMS WhatsApp.
  • Staff rota or at least footfall and transactions by hour.
  • Age check log refusals and training record.

Start with POS exports. Keep customer data private and only use what you need for each step.

Metrics to Track ROI

  • Average basket value and items per basket.
  • Stockout rate on top 100 SKUs and on chilled singles.
  • Waste rate on short date chilled lines.
  • Gross margin after promos and supplier deals.
  • Revenue per labour hour and queue time at peak.
  • Age check pass rate refusals logged and retraining done.

Example 30 Day Focus

  • Week 1: add an FAQ assistant to your site. Turn on basket add on prompts for mixers snacks and ice. Put a join QR for WhatsApp at the counter.
  • Week 2: load 26 weeks of POS and stock. Enable forecasts for top 300 SKUs. Create a basic reorder list and low stock alerts for beer wine spirits and soft drinks.
  • Week 3: switch on short date discounts two hours before close for chilled lines. Set event packs for match nights and bank holidays.
  • Week 4: review basket size stockouts waste margin and queue time versus last month. Keep the two best wins.

What good looks like

  • Average basket up by 6 to 12 percent.
  • Stockouts on hero SKUs down by 25 to 40 percent.
  • Waste on chilled down by 20 to 35 percent.
  • Margin protected with better mix and fewer panic discounts.
  • Shorter queues on weekend peak due to better rotas.

Keep it simple. One change per week. Measure then scale what works.

Set up in under an hour

  • Export POS sales and stock to CSV.
  • Switch on WhatsApp Business or an SMS sender and add a join link at the counter.
  • Add the assistants to your site with clear CTAs on opening hours and delivery pages.

If you use EPOS Now Lightspeed Shopify Square Vend or Clover start with built in reports and simple webhooks.

Fast wins to test

  • Mixer add on prompt with gin vodka and rum.
  • Snack bundle at checkout crisps nuts chocolate with a small perk.
  • Quiet hour offer midweek on low and no alcohol or premium mixers.
  • Short date price drops on chilled singles two hours before close.

Guardrails

  • Get opt in for messages and make opt out easy.
  • Don’t market alcohol to minors. Use age check prompts and follow Challenge 25 or your local scheme.
  • Keep customer data private and limit who can access it.
  • Follow licensing hours price display and promo rules in your area.

Integration Pathways

  • POS and commerce: Shopify Lightspeed Square Vend EPOS Now and Clover export CSV and support APIs.
  • Messaging: WhatsApp Business and SMS gateways for reminders offers and service updates.
  • Payments: links for click and collect deposits and event packs.
  • Dashboards: Google Sheets Looker Studio or Power BI for weekly review.

Go step by step. You won’t need to replace your POS to start.

AI Build Options

  • Low code: n8n or Zapier for refill flows reorder alerts and offer sends.
  • Forecasts: simple models from past sales weather promos sports and events.
  • Rules: an approved answer bank for age checks delivery ID accepted returns and licensing queries.
  • Till prompts: a light add on script for mixers snacks ice and cups.

Keep messages short clear and linked to one action.

Staff Workflow Impact

  • Fewer Where is my order and opening hours questions at the counter.
  • Better rotas from footfall and promo plans so peaks don’t overwhelm tills.
  • Simple age check prompts and logs reduce mistakes.
  • Managers spend more time on display and service not spreadsheets.

Cost vs Return

  • +12 add on items per day @ £2.00 is about £720 per month.
  • Stockouts down on hero lines protects hundreds per month in lost sales.
  • Waste down £10 per day on chilled is about £300 per month.

Most tools cost far less than the gains above.

Customer Experience Gains

  • Clear helpful advice not jargon.
  • Fewer empty shelves on favourites at peak.
  • Faster service with the right rota.
  • Simple answers online for delivery ID accepted opening hours and returns.

Retailer Readiness Checklist

  • POS exports for sales stock and discounts.
  • Supplier lead times case sizes and delivery days on file.
  • WhatsApp Business or an SMS sender set up.
  • Named owner for a weekly results review.

If you tick most of these you can start this week.

Future Plays 12 to 24 months

  • Computer vision shelf checks for gaps and facing.
  • Dynamic pricing on short date stock within safe rules.
  • Personalised offers based on baskets not profiles.
  • Handheld scans that keep stock live with fewer counts.

Case Example

  • Single store near a stadium.
  • Turned on weather and event forecasts plus mixer prompts.
  • Basket up 10 percent in four weeks.
  • Stockouts down 30 percent on lager and mixers.
  • Waste on chilled dropped with timed markdowns.

Results will vary. The weekly review locks in wins and drops weak ideas.
