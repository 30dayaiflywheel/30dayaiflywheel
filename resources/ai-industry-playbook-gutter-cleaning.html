Gutter Cleaning AI Playbook

Fill the diary raise average job value and reduce callouts

What this page gives you

This playbook shows where AI helps a gutter cleaning business today. It focuses on steady bookings smarter seasonal reminders quick photo quotes and clear add ons that lift average job value while cutting wasted travel and callbacks.

Use the 30 Day AI Flywheel: Identify Cycle Measure Scale.

Typical Growth Drivers

  • Increase bookings for single homes blocks and small commercial sites.
  • Lift add ons for fascia and soffit wash downpipe flush minor seal checks and gutter guard install.
  • Reduce no shows and late cancels with deposits and helpful prep reminders.
  • Shorten quote time with photo and address based estimates.
  • Raise repeat work with seasonal prompts after heavy rain and autumn leaf fall.

AI Use Cases Already Working

  • Booking assistant on web and WhatsApp that checks postcode access notes height and preferred time slots.
  • Instant quote helper that reads images street view and roofline to estimate meters and complexity then builds a package.
  • Smart reminders with prep tips move cars unlock gates pet safety and ladder or vac access plus easy reschedule.
  • After care explainer with what we found what we fixed and when to check again with photos for proof.
  • Route optimiser that clusters jobs by area roof height and gear required to add one more job per day.
  • Review and referral booster with before and after photos and simple links.
  • Answer bank for leaks overflow and staining with safe first steps and when to book a repair specialist.

Data Readiness What you need

  • Job history address height method vac or hand meters cleared time on site and status.
  • Lead and quote logs with source photos price and won or lost reason.
  • Customer list with postcode consent and preferred channel.
  • Calendar travel time crew gear and weather notes.
  • Reviews with rating text and media where allowed.

Start with CSV exports. Keep customer data private and only use what you need for each step.

Metrics to Track ROI

  • Lead to job rate and time to first reply.
  • Average job value and add on attach rate.
  • No show and late cancel rate with deposit coverage.
  • Jobs per day and travel per job.
  • Callback rate within 14 days and reasons found.
  • Review score and referrals generated.

Example 30 Day Focus

  • Week 1: add a booking and quote assistant on site and WhatsApp. Turn on confirmations deposits and reminder messages with easy reschedule.
  • Week 2: load last 12 months of jobs quotes and reviews. Enable photo quotes using address and front shot as a minimum.
  • Week 3: publish simple bundles clear only clear plus fascia wash clear plus fascia and downpipe flush with what is included.
  • Week 4: review lead to job rate average job value no shows jobs per day callback rate and reviews versus last month. Keep the best two wins.

What good looks like

  • Lead to job rate up by 15 to 25 percent.
  • Average job value up by 10 to 20 percent from add ons.
  • No shows down by 20 to 40 percent with deposits and reminders.
  • Jobs per day up through tighter routing and fewer gaps.
  • Callback rate down as photos and notes set expectations.

Keep it simple. One change per week. Measure then scale what works.

Set up in under an hour

  • Export jobs quotes and customers to CSV with dates property type and height.
  • Switch on confirmations deposits and reminders for bookings.
  • Add the assistant to your site and WhatsApp with clear call to action and a QR on flyers and vans.

If you use Calendly Stripe Google Calendar or a job manager you can start with built in reports and message tools.

Fast wins to test

  • Rain week gap fill texts for streets you already serviced.
  • Autumn leaf fall campaign with neighborhood bundles.
  • Fascia and soffit wash add on at a small perk for repeat customers.
  • After care tips with next suggested check based on trees and roof pitch.

Guardrails

  • Do not give structural advice by message. If you suspect damage recommend a roofer or builder visit.
  • Follow ladder and roof safety and weather rules. If wind is high or surfaces are icy don’t proceed and reschedule.
  • Keep addresses and photos private and restrict who can access them.
  • Get consent for messages and make opt out easy.
  • Document findings and disclaimers on the job record with photos.

Integration Pathways

  • Scheduling: Calendly Google Calendar Outlook.
  • Routing: Google Maps and route tools with area clustering.
  • Messaging: WhatsApp Business SMS and email.
  • Payments: Stripe deposit links and pay on completion.
  • Reviews: Google and Facebook with photo requests.

Adopt step by step. You don’t need to replace current tools to start.

AI Build Options

  • Low code: n8n or Zapier for lead capture reminders and review asks.
  • Chat layer: a guarded assistant that quotes from your price table and access rules.
  • Vision: photo intake plus street view to estimate meters and height band.
  • Dashboards: Google Sheets Looker Studio or Power BI for weekly review.

Keep messages short clear and tied to one action.

Staff Workflow Impact

  • Fewer back and forth quotes as photos and rules set expectations.
  • Better day plans with clustered routes and gear notes.
  • Less time on the phone and more time on paid work.
  • Clear after care reduces repeat visits for issues that aren’t faults.

Cost vs Return

  • Lead to job up 20 percent lifts revenue without more ads.
  • No shows down 30 percent protects full day value.
  • Average job value up from fascia wash and downpipe flush add ons.

Most tools cost far less than the gains above.

Customer Experience Gains

  • Fast quotes with clear options and honest limits.
  • Simple reschedule and deposit links that don’t need a call.
  • On time arrivals thanks to smart routing and weather plans.
  • Photo proof of work with notes on next check.

Business Readiness Checklist

  • 12 months of job and quote data exportable to CSV.
  • Price table by meters height access and add ons.
  • Template messages for prep after care and reviews.
  • WhatsApp Business or SMS sender set up.
  • Named owner for weekly review of results.

If you tick most of these you can start this week.

Future Plays 12 to 24 months

  • Voice notes to job record and automatic photo tagging.
  • Seasonal demand forecasting to plan offers routes and crew size.
  • Vision models to detect sag broken clips and overflow risk.
  • Dynamic pricing that balances travel time and slot fill rate.
  • Neighborhood drops that auto build lists from completed jobs.

Case Example

  • Three person crew covering a county.
  • Turned on photo quotes deposits and smart reminders.
  • Lead to job up 21 percent in six weeks.
  • No shows down 35 percent and jobs per day up by 17 percent.
  • Average job value up 14 percent with fascia wash and flush add ons.

Results will vary. The weekly review locks in wins and drops weak ideas.
