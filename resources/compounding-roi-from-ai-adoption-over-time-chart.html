Timeline of Early vs Late Adopters ROI

ROI does not grow in a straight line. It compounds with each 30 day cycle. This chart shows how the gap widens when you start early.

What you get

  • Make the cost of waiting visible to your leadership team.

How the timeline works

  • Month 1–3: Early adopters run first cycles and prove value in small areas. Late adopters are still researching.
  • Month 4–6: Early adopters scale first wins and start more cycles. ROI climbs. Late adopters are shortlisting tools.
  • Month 7–12: Early adopters have several cycles done and a set of proven use cases. ROI compounds as gains stack. Late adopters may be on their first cycle.
  • Year 2: The gap is clear. Early adopters cut costs and lift experience. Late adopters face a harder climb against mature AI processes.

What the diagram shows

  • A rising curve for early adopters that steepens as cycles complete.
  • A flat line for late adopters in year one, then a slow rise.
  • A widening ROI gap over time.

Lesson

Value comes from compounding. Each cycle adds knowledge, data, and adoption that you cannot buy later. Delay does not just slow you down, it risks leaving you behind.
