Podiatrists and Foot Clinics – AI Playbook

Fill the diary, grow orthotics revenue, and improve adherence

What this page gives you

This playbook shows where AI helps a podiatry clinic today. It focuses on steady bookings, better home care follow through, and simple ways to lift orthotics and package sales without pressure.

Use the 30 Day AI Flywheel: Identify Cycle Measure Scale.

Typical Growth Drivers

  • Increase new assessments and fill follow ups.
  • Lift acceptance of care plans and orthotics where suitable.
  • Reduce no shows and late cancellations.
  • Grow package revenue nail surgery, verruca care, diabetic foot checks.

AI Use Cases Already Working

  • Booking assistant for assessments, follow ups, nail care, and shockwave on web and WhatsApp.
  • Symptom triage that routes high risk cases diabetic, infected, wound to a clinician call back.
  • Care plan explainer simple language on visits, orthotics options, and time lines.
  • Exercise and footwear coach sends short clips and reminders and logs completion.
  • Automated review prompts and outcome check ins at 2 and 6 weeks.

Data Readiness (What you need)

  • Appointment history with type, status, and attendance.
  • Care plan and procedure logs including orthotics ordered.
  • Retail POS for footwear, insoles, and treatment packs.
  • Patient CRM with consent flags and basic risk tags diabetic, anticoagulant.

CSV exports are fine to start. Keep health data private and only use what is needed for each step.

Metrics to Track ROI

  • Diary fill rate and no show rate.
  • Assessment to follow up conversion.
  • Care plan acceptance and orthotics attach rate.
  • Average revenue per patient and package mix.
  • Reviews per month and average rating.

Example 30 Day Focus

  • Week 1: add a booking assistant and turn on confirmations and reminders with easy reschedule links.
  • Week 2: load last 12 weeks of appointments. Enable quiet day gap fill texts for same day assessments and nail care.
  • Week 3: add a care plan and orthotics explainer with simple options and finance if you offer it. Start exercise and footwear coach messages.
  • Week 4: review fill rate, no shows, care plan acceptance, orthotics attach rate, and reviews vs last month. Keep the best two wins.

What “good” looks like

  • No shows down by 15 to 30 percent.
  • Assessment to follow up conversion up by 8 to 15 points.
  • Orthotics attach rate rising with clearer guidance.
  • More recent reviews and higher rating.
  • Fewer re attend issues thanks to better home care adherence.

Keep it simple. One change per week. Measure, then scale what works.

Set up in under an hour

  • Export appointments, care plan logs, and POS to CSV.
  • Switch on confirmations and reminders for all bookings.
  • Add the assistant to your site and WhatsApp with clear CTAs and a QR at reception.

If you use Cliniko, TM3, Jane, or Power Diary, start with built in reports and message tools.

Fast wins to test

  • Quiet day gap fill text for same day nail care and follow ups.
  • Two week check in for orthotics comfort and simple bedding in tips.
  • Diabetic foot check campaign to lapsed patients before winter.

Guardrails

  • Do not give clinical advice by message. Offer a quick call with a podiatrist for concerns.
  • Keep patient data private and limit who can access it.
  • Get consent for messages and make opt out easy.
  • Record advice given where required by your regulator or insurer.
