Bars and Pubs – AI Playbook

Lift footfall, boost spend per head, and cut waste

What this page gives you

This playbook shows where AI helps a bar or pub today. It focuses on steady footfall, stronger event nights, and better margins.

Use the 30 Day AI Flywheel: Identify Cycle Measure Scale.

Typical Growth Drivers

  • Fill quiet early week slots with targeted offers.
  • Raise spend per head with simple pairings and bundles.
  • Improve stock planning to cut waste and avoid outages.
  • Promote events quiz night, sport, live music to the right people.

AI Use Cases Already Working

  • AI booking and enquiry assistant on web and WhatsApp.
  • Demand forecasting from weather and nearby events to plan staff and stock.
  • Dynamic specials and bundles based on surplus or expected demand.
  • Personalised SMS or email nudges for locals and past guests.
  • Auto send review prompts and quick reply drafts to keep tone on brand.

Data Readiness (What you need)

  • POS history by hour and category drinks, food, bundles.
  • Event calendar sport fixtures, quiz nights, live music.
  • Staff rota and simple stock logs.
  • Basic CRM or contact list with consent flags.

CSV export is fine to start. Add integrations later if you want full automation.

Metrics to Track ROI

  • Covers or transactions per hour by day of week.
  • Spend per head and attach rate for food or premium drinks.
  • Waste percentage on draft and perishables.
  • Event night occupancy and pre bookings.
  • Repeat visit rate from locals within 30 days.

Example 30 Day Focus

  • Week 1: add an AI assistant for bookings and enquiries. Turn on confirmations and reminder messages for group tables and events.
  • Week 2: create a locals list within 1 km and send one mid week offer that does not devalue the brand small bundle or early evening happy hour window.
  • Week 3: pilot demand forecasting for a big match or live music night to plan staff and stock and set dynamic specials.
  • Week 4: review transactions per hour, spend per head, waste, and event occupancy vs last month. Keep the top two changes.

What “good” looks like

  • Mid week transactions up by 5 to 12 percent.
  • Spend per head up by £1.50 to £4.00.
  • Waste down by 10 to 20 percent.
  • Event nights closer to full with fewer stock outs.

Keep it simple. One change per week. Measure, then scale what works.

Set up in under an hour

  • Export last 12 weeks of POS by hour and category.
  • Switch on confirmations and reminders for bookings and events.
  • Add the assistant to your site and WhatsApp with clear CTAs.

If you use Square, Lightspeed, Toast or similar, start with built in features.

Fast wins to test

  • Early evening bundle pint plus small plate for a fixed price.
  • Weather aware promos cold night stout and hot snacks, warm day spritz and sharing boards.
  • Auto send review link next morning after a booking.

Guardrails

  • Get consent for messages and make opt out easy.
  • Do not over discount. Use time windows and bundles instead.
  • Keep data inside your POS and booking tools.
