Health and Beauty Retail AI Playbook

Grow basket size increase repeat visits reduce stockouts and cut admin

What this page gives you

This playbook shows where AI helps a health and beauty retailer today. It focuses on bigger baskets, more loyal customers, fewer stockouts on fast lines, and quicker answers that cut phone and counter time.

Use the 30 Day AI Flywheel: Identify Cycle Measure Scale.

Typical Growth Drivers

  • Increase average basket with simple add ons at till and online.
  • Lift repeat visits with refills reminders and service booking.
  • Reduce stockouts on hero SKUs hair skin fragrance vitamins.
  • Cut waste on short life items testers and gift sets.
  • Use staff time better with cleaner rotas and fewer routine queries.

AI Use Cases Already Working

  • Demand forecasts by SKU and store to guide orders and space.
  • Replen alerts that factor lead times and promo uplifts.
  • Consultation assistant skin hair quiz with product matches.
  • Offer engine for basket add ons masks tools minis and gifts.
  • Refill reminders by WhatsApp SMS or email based on purchase date.
  • FAQ assistant for returns ingredients allergens and services like brow bar or piercings.

Data Readiness What you need

  • POS sales by date time SKU price quantity discount and channel.
  • On hand stock by SKU with last delivery and supplier lead time.
  • Promo calendar and content banners offers and sampling.
  • Customer list with consent flags for email SMS WhatsApp.
  • Staff rota or at least footfall and transactions by hour.
  • Ingredients list and allergens for compliance questions.

Start with POS exports. Keep customer data private and only use what you need for each step.

Metrics to Track ROI

  • Average basket value and items per basket.
  • Stockout rate on top 100 SKUs.
  • Promo sell through and halo uplift on linked items.
  • Repeat rate within 45 and 90 days by category.
  • Revenue per labour hour and queue time at peak.
  • Service conversion consults to sales and rebooking.

Example 30 Day Focus

  • Week 1: add an FAQ and consultation assistant on site. Turn on refill reminders for vitamins and skincare. Add a join QR for WhatsApp at till.
  • Week 2: load 26 weeks of POS and stock. Enable forecasts for top 300 SKUs. Create a basic reorder list and low stock alerts for hero lines.
  • Week 3: switch on basket add on prompts masks tools minis at till and checkout. Launch a quiet time offer midweek on services.
  • Week 4: review basket size stockouts repeat rate and service bookings versus last month. Keep the two best wins.

What good looks like

  • Average basket up by 6 to 12 percent.
  • Stockouts on hero SKUs down by 25 to 40 percent.
  • Repeat rate up by 8 to 15 points on refill categories.
  • Service bookings up without heavy discounting.
  • Shorter queues at peak due to better rotas.

Keep it simple. One change per week. Measure then scale what works.

Set up in under an hour

  • Export POS sales and stock to CSV.
  • Switch on WhatsApp Business or an SMS sender and add a join link at the counter.
  • Add the assistants to your site with clear CTAs on consultation pages.

If you use EPOS Now Lightspeed Shopify Square or Vend start with built in reports and simple webhooks.

Fast wins to test

  • Refill nudge for vitamins and cleanser at 28 to 35 days.
  • Bundle add on at checkout brush sponge cotton pads with a small perk.
  • Quiet hour service slots midweek with a soft incentive.
  • Allergy and ingredient checker link in receipts and product pages.

Guardrails

  • Get opt in for messages and make opt out easy.
  • Don’t claim medical outcomes. Keep guidance as general advice and signpost a pharmacist or clinician when needed.
  • Keep customer data private and limit who can access it.
  • Follow pricing display and cosmetics labelling rules in your area.

Integration Pathways

  • POS and commerce: Shopify Lightspeed Square Vend and EPOS Now export CSV and support APIs.
  • Messaging: WhatsApp Business and SMS gateways for reminders offers and service updates.
  • Payments: links for deposits click and collect and service prepay.
  • Dashboards: Google Sheets Looker Studio or Power BI for weekly review.

Go step by step. You won’t need to replace your POS to start.

AI Build Options

  • Low code: n8n or Zapier for refill flows reorder alerts and offer sends.
  • Forecasts: simple models from past sales seasonality promos and events.
  • Rules: an approved answer bank for ingredients allergens returns and services.
  • Till prompts: a light add on script for common accessories and minis.

Keep messages short clear and linked to one action.

Staff Workflow Impact

  • Fewer Where is my order and ingredients questions at the counter.
  • Consultation notes saved so staff don’t need to repeat long Q and A.
  • Better rotas from footfall and promo plans so peaks don’t overwhelm tills.
  • Managers spend more time on display and service not spreadsheets.

Cost vs Return

  • +12 add on items per day @ £2.50 is about £900 per month.
  • Stockouts down on hero lines protects hundreds per month in lost sales.
  • Repeat rate up 10 points compounds revenue over the quarter.

Most tools cost far less than the gains above.

Customer Experience Gains

  • Clear helpful advice not jargon.
  • Fewer empty shelves on favourites.
  • Faster service at peak with the right rota.
  • Simple answers online for returns ingredients allergens and services.

Retailer Readiness Checklist

  • POS exports for sales stock and discounts.
  • Supplier lead times case sizes and delivery days on file.
  • WhatsApp Business or an SMS sender set up.
  • Named owner for a weekly results review.

If you tick most of these you can start this week.

Future Plays 12 to 24 months

  • Computer vision shelf checks for gaps and planogram compliance.
  • Dynamic pricing on short life stock within safe rules.
  • Personalised offers based on baskets not profiles.
  • Handheld scans that keep stock live with fewer counts.

Case Example

  • Two store chain in a busy town.
  • Turned on refill reminders and basket add on prompts.
  • Basket up 9 percent in four weeks.
  • Stockouts down 33 percent on hero lines with better reorder timing.
  • Service bookings grew without heavy discounting.

Results will vary. The weekly review locks in wins and drops weak ideas.
